About me

Need a Computer Technician? I can Help You!

Hi, I’m Elde Arotcha, an IT Support Specialist based in Manila, Philippines with 7 years of hands-on technical experience in hardware, software, and network troubleshooting. I specialize in diagnosing and resolving Windows-based system issues, performing hardware repairs, configuring networks, and providing end-user technical support for home users and small businesses. My background combines technical expertise with strong communication skills, allowing me to explain complex technical issues in simple, non-technical language.

Recently, I completed a paid technical trial with the IT Department of MedGrocer, where I handled real-time corporate IT operations including laptop deployment, Windows 11 installations, hardware component replacement (RAM, SSD, screens, keyboards), ticketing systems (ManageEngine, Airtable), Wi-Fi monitoring, and daily system checks (CCTV, printers, Smart TVs).
